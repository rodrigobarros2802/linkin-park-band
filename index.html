about the band

Linkin Park is an American rock band formed in Agoura Hills, California. The band's current lineup includes vocalist and multi-instrumentalist Mike Shinoda, guitarist Brad Delson, bassist Dave Farrell, DJ Joe Hahn, and drummer Rob Bourdon, all founding members. Another member who had been with the band since its founding was Chester Bennington, who died in 2017.
